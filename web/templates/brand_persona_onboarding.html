Welcome to Brand Persona Training

This onboarding process will help us understand your brand's unique voice, tone, and style so we can replicate it perfectly in all content generation.

Choose Your Path:

  • Quick (5 questions) - Covers 80% of your brand voice in ~5 minutes. Perfect for getting started fast.
  • Extended (20 questions) - Next most important questions. Complete after the quick version for deeper persona.
  • Full (100 questions) - Comprehensive interview covering all aspects. Takes ~60 minutes but creates the most complete persona.

Key Principles:

  • Be Specific - Instead of "I like clarity," tell us "I never use 'leverage' as a verb, I keep paragraphs under 3 sentences"
  • Focus on Rejections - 80% of your persona will be what you'd NEVER say or do
  • Answer Honestly - This is your brand's DNA. Be authentic, not what you think sounds good.
